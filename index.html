HELLO,

I'm Attila Krüpl, C++ software engineer, web enthusiast, science addict.

I can be the part time team member of your developer team if you are looking for someone with experience in C++/Qt software development or web development.

If you are a student or you are going to high school I can help you to prepare for your math and statistics exams.

In 2009 I graduated as a lawyer. Since then I gained a significant experience in managing people in Great Britain, learned IT with engineering at the University of Szeged, learned web development at Green Fox Academy - Budapest, and did a plenty of other trainings and courses to become a software engineer.

For more information about my experience, studies and interests please see my profile below.
